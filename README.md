# [Play Aura Monster Simulator](https://raw.githack.com/DavideFocalors79/AuraMonster/refs/heads/main/aura_monster.html)
Are you a true Sigma or just an NPC in someone else’s story? AURA MONSTER is a high-stakes, narrative-driven "Aura Simulator" where every choice defines your social standing in the universe. Inspired by the "Sigma" internet subculture and the tense atmosphere of Re:Zero, this game challenges you to maintain absolute stoicism in the face of cosmic, professional, and paranormal absurdity.

A meteorite is heading for your house? Finish your pizza. An alien lands in your backyard? Offer it a coffee and drink it yourself. In AURA MONSTER, the goal isn’t just to survive—it’s to remain completely unbothered.
